NBA Finals Champions

2020 NBA Finals

Which teams will play in the 2020 NBA Finals? Fans think the Clippers, Lakers, Raptors, Celtics, and Bucks all have a chance to be playing in June. The teams playing in the Finals will be decided during the NBA Playoffs, which are set at the completion of the 2019-2020 NBA regular-season. Until that time, you can purchase NBA tickets for all regular-season games. Once the playoffs are set, we will also have NBA playoffs tickets and even NBA Finals tickets available to purchase.
