Depends on who you are asking.

There 193 official UN member countries, and 2 observer states, the Holy See (Vatican City) and Palestine.

Some countries don't recognize some offical memebers. For example, North and South Korea do not recognize each other. Many countries do not recognize Israel, 30 in total.

Some places declare themselves independent, like Kosovo, North Cyprus, Taiwan, and ect.

So, you can form your own conclusion. But there 193 offical memebers of the UN in 2019.
