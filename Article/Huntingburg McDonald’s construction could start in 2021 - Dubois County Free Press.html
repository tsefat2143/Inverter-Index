Huntingburg McDonald’s construction could start in 2021

February 28, 2020 by Local Sources 5 Comments

Construction on the McDonald’s Restaurant in Huntingburg is expected to begin in 2021.

The new restaurant will be located on the southwest corner of U.S. 231 and State Road 64 across from CVS. According to the property owner, the company began leasing the property this year and expects to break ground early next year.

The company filed for building permits for the new location in 2018.

The story originally stated they were seeking a franchisee for the new location but according to a McDonald’s spokesperson, this was incorrect. The story and headline have been changed to reflect that information.
