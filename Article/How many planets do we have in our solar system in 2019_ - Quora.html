Officially, according to the International Astronomical Union’s definition of what a “planet” is - there are eight:

Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune.

HOWEVER:

Before the IAU’s official ruling, most people would have said nine - including Pluto. If you look further into the past, there was a time when Ceres (now rated as a “dwarf plant”) was also considered to be a planet.

Some people dislike the IAU’s definition - and wish to add Pluto back onto the list…but if you do that, then why not also include Ceres?
